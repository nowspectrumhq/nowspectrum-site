The story behind NowSpectrum.

What is NowSpectrum?

NowSpectrum is a ServiceNow knowledge brand. We create practical, no-fluff resources for ServiceNow developers, admins, and ITSM professionals — cheat sheets, reference guides, certification prep packs, and interview kits.

Everything published here is written from real, hands-on ServiceNow experience. Not recycled documentation. Not theoretical examples. Actual patterns and solutions from production environments.

Why we built this

If you have ever worked in ServiceNow, you know the problem. You are trying to remember the exact syntax for an encoded query, or you need to quickly check how OAuth 2.0 Client Credentials works, or you are preparing for an interview the next morning — and you end up spending 30 minutes Googling the same things you have looked up a dozen times before.

NowSpectrum exists to fix that. Every guide is built to be the resource you wish existed when you started — clear, concise, immediately useful.

Who is behind NowSpectrum?

NowSpectrum is built by a ServiceNow developer with 3+ years of hands-on production experience across ITSM, HRSD, REST integrations, Flow Designer, and platform development. The content comes directly from that work — the tips, the mistakes, the patterns that actually hold up in the real world.

What we cover

GlideRecord scripting, Flow Designer, REST integrations, OAuth 2.0, MID Server, Import Sets, ACLs and security, Now Assist AI, performance optimisation, Update Sets, interview preparation, and certification study — with more being added regularly.
